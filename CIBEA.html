By looking at the energy statistics of Pakistan, almost 70-80 % of our energy supply is being wasted. Much of this waste is recoverable by deploying energy efficiency, energy conservation and waste heat recovery measures. A MW saved is a MW generated, however this savings can be achieved at a fractional cost as compared to installing additional generation capacity. The first step to realize these savings is conducting an energy audit.
